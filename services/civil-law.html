Civil Law in Lebanon

Clear advice for civil claims and private disputes

Civil law governs many of the legal relationships that arise between individuals, families, professionals and businesses. These matters may involve contractual obligations, unpaid debts, compensation claims, liability, property rights or disputes resulting from private agreements.

Abed Al Karim Hijazi Law Office assists clients in assessing their legal position, reviewing available documents and identifying the most appropriate course of action. The firm seeks practical solutions where negotiation is possible and provides determined representation when court proceedings are necessary.

Our civil law services may include

  • Contractual disputes and enforcement of obligations
  • Claims for compensation and civil liability
  • Debt recovery and payment disputes
  • Review of agreements and supporting documents
  • Negotiation and amicable settlement
  • Representation before Lebanese courts

A practical and strategic approach

Every civil dispute requires careful examination of the facts, evidence and applicable legal rules. The firm explains the available options clearly and helps clients evaluate the potential benefits, risks and costs of each strategy.
